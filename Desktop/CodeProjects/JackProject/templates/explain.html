O/U Formula (Estimated Total Runs)

Away team runs = Away Offense (R/G) × (Home SP ERA ÷ 4.20) × Park Factor

Home team runs = Home Offense (R/G) × (Away SP ERA ÷ 4.20) × Park Factor

SP adj above 1.0 means a worse-than-average pitcher — more runs expected. Park factor >1.0 means a hitter-friendly park (e.g. Coors Field = 1.23×).
