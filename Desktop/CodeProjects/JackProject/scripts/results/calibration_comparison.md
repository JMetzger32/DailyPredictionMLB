## Verdict
- **A vs B (identical 2025 rows): the leak fix IMPROVES calibration — ECE 0.0313 → 0.0266 (−0.0047).**
- The live OLD model (D, ECE 0.0795) is ~2.5–3× worse-calibrated than either holdout. The leaked
  holdout (A) flatters the model because its 2025 test rows carry the same leak, which does not
  hold out-of-sample in 2026.
- The leak-fixed 2026 approximation (C, ECE 0.0400) is ~2× better-calibrated than the live OLD
  model (D), though C uses pipeline prior-season SP inputs rather than the live snapshot, so treat
  it as indicative, not exact.
- Low-probability bins in C/D have tiny counts (n≤6) and are noisy; weight the 40–80% bins.
