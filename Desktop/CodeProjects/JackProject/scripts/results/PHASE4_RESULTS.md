# Phase 4b — leak-fix holdout results (2025 holdout, train 2021–2024)

LR+GB ensemble AUC / LogLoss / Brier. Reduced = SP + bullpen-ERA features dropped.
Generated by `scripts/leak_test_holdout.py` (before and after the Phase 4a change).

| Config | AUC | LogLoss | Brier | AUC vs no-SP |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **2b** leaked FULL (same-season SP/bullpen) | 0.6083 | 0.6719 | 0.2396 | +0.0514 |
| **4b** prior-season FULL (leak-free)        | 0.5762 | 0.6813 | 0.2442 | +0.0193 |
| REDUCED (no SP/bullpen at all)              | 0.5569 | 0.6852 | 0.2461 | —       |

## Reading
- **Leak inflation:** 0.6083 → 0.5762 = **0.0321 AUC** of the SP/bullpen contribution was
  look-ahead leakage (same-season aggregates include games after each row's date).
- **Genuine pre-game signal retained:** 0.5762 − 0.5569 = **+0.0193 AUC** above a no-SP
  model. Still > 0.01, so the SP/bullpen features are worth keeping — just at their honest,
  leak-free value, not the inflated 0.0514.
- Consistent with the live 2026 calibration (scripts/calibration_report.py): the leaked
  holdout (~0.61 AUC) did not translate to live accuracy (53.5%, over-confident).

## Status of the live artifact

OLD leaked features (and the removed 1.4× boost). It could not be regenerated locally
